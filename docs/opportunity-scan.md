## Opportunity Discovery Table
| No. | Observed Problem | Target User | Current Alternative | Possible IT Solution | Feasible Technology |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Students miss academic deadlines due to notifications being scattered across LMS, emails, and chats. | University students | Manual calendar notes, desktop screenshots | Consolidated deadline reminder dashboard | HTML/CSS/JS frontend + mock JSON data framework |
| 2 | Small campus cafes experience stock-outs and tracking delays due to manual log handling. | Campus cafe owners & staff | Paper notebook ledgers, physical inventory memory | Simple stock-alert inventory manager app | Google Forms data ingestion + Google Sheets dashboard |
| 3 | Students lose personal items and lack a central search timeline or unified digital claim desk. | University students & campus security | Chat groups, checking security office manually | Centralized campus lost-and-found board | Landing page form + Airtable inventory collection |
| 4 | On-campus clinic queues overflow physically because students cannot monitor live waiting spaces. | University students & clinic staff | Physical wait queues, phone check-ins | Live queue tracking and booking portal mockup | Figma clickable wireframes + Google Sheets backend simulation |
| 5 | Students struggle to find peers for casual language exchange or niche code debugging practice. | University students | Unstructured Discord channels, bulleting boards | Peer-learning skill-matching directory app | Bubble.io or dynamic HTML text search filtering layout |
| 6 | Student club events face high check-in delays due to manual paper registration rosters. | Club organizers & event attendees | Manual paper sign-up sheets, excel entry | Digital check-in tracker & attendance log system | Web app registration form linked directly to Airtable database |
